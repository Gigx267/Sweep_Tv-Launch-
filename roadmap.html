📅 2025: Foundation & Reorganisation
July 24, 2025 NEC MEETING

NEC Constitutes Convention Planning Committee

At its July meeting, the National Executive Committee sets the stage for a December elective convention. The push for a "full NWC shake-up" gains momentum to accommodate new governors and returning members. [citation:3]

• Article 12 (4) lists 22 NWC positions • Tenure: 4 years (eligible for re-election) [citation:3]
August 13, 2025 ZONAL STRATEGY

South-west Announces 2,000 Ward Canvassers

Zonal Chairman Isaacs Kekemeke unveils plan to appoint dedicated canvassers in all 2,000 wards across the zone to begin grassroots sensitisation for Tinubu's re-election. "South-west must do for Tinubu what North-west did for Buhari." [citation:5]

December 2025 MEMBERSHIP DRIVE

Nationwide Digital e-Registration Begins

APC launches digital membership registration with NIN verification. The exercise runs through January 2026, capturing demographic data for planning and grassroots mobilisation. Early reports show 20% of registrants are from North-west. [citation:8][citation:9]

📅 2026: Congresses, Conventions & Primaries
January 20, 2026 CAMPAIGN KICK-OFF

"We're Ready for Campaign" – Yilwatda

At a youth mobilisation event, National Chairman declares that all appointees are politicians and must participate. "North Central will take over" vote delivery in 2027, while North-west boasts of exceeding 2023 numbers. [citation:8]

January 30, 2026 REGISTRATION DEADLINE

Digital e-Registration Concludes

Membership update ends with significant youth participation. Data to guide ward-level planning and target setting. [citation:9]

February 2, 2026 CONGRESS NOTICES

Official Congress Timetable Released

Notices issued for Ward and Local Government congresses. Sale of forms: Feb 4-9. Screening: Feb 10-13. [citation:6][citation:9]

February 18-20, 2026 WARD & LG CONGRESSES

Ward Congresses (Feb 18), LG Congresses (Feb 20)

Election of ward executives and local government executives. LG congresses also elect three delegates (one female) to National Convention. Appeals: Feb 21-22. [citation:6][citation:9]

February 24, 2026 STRATEGY SUMMIT

Uzodimma, Yilwatda Unveil "3-Pillar Communication Architecture"

At State House summit, PGF chairman warns of "communication failure". New framework links Renewed Hope Ambassadors, govt info managers, and party structures. Focus: bridging gap between reforms and grassroots perception. [citation:1]

March 7, 2026 STATE CONGRESSES

Election of State Executives

State congresses hold across 36 states. Appeals: March 9-11. [citation:6][citation:9]

March 21, 2026 ZONAL CONGRESSES

Six Geo-Political Zones Elect Zonal Officers

Venues: Ibadan (SW), Enugu (SE), Rivers/Cross River (SS), Kaduna (NW), Gombe/Bauchi (NE), Nasarawa (NC). Appeals: March 23. [citation:9]

March 25-28, 2026 NATIONAL CONVENTION

APC Elects New National Working Committee

Four-day convention marks the climax of internal party elections. New NWC members will lead the party into 2027 general elections. [citation:6][citation:9]

April 2026 DEFECTION WATCH

Mass Defections Expected as Primaries Approach

Opposition parties (PDP, ADC, LP) prepare to receive APC members denied tickets. INEC fixed party primaries for April 23 - May 30, 2026. Lawmakers fear governors will impose candidates. [citation:2]

April 23 - May 30, 2026 PRIMARIES

APC Primaries for All Elective Positions

INEC window for party primaries: 109 senatorial, 360 federal constituencies, 990 state assembly seats. Direct primaries mandated by Electoral Act. [citation:2]

June 20, 2026 TEST RUN: EKITI GOV

Ekiti Governorship Election

Senate Leader Opeyemi Bamidele chairs campaign council. Target: 500,000 votes for Oyebanji. Described as "referendum" on APC performance and precursor to 2027. [citation:4][citation:10]

August 8, 2026 OSUN TARGET

Osun APC Targets 1 Million Votes for Tinubu

Renewed Hope Ambassadors launch local govt structures to deliver governorship candidate and build toward 1 million presidential votes from Osun. [citation:7]

📅 2027: General Elections
January 16 - February 6, 2027 GENERAL ELECTIONS WINDOW

INEC Revised Timetable

Following Electoral Act 2026, INEC realigned election dates. Presidential and NASS elections: February 20, 2027 (original) but adjusted to Jan-Feb window. [citation:2][citation:10]

March 6, 2027 GUBERNATORIAL & STATE HOUSES

Elections in 36 States and FCT

APC aims to retain majority and expand control, leveraging "Renewed Hope" reforms and grassroots machinery. [citation:2]
