About Sweep TV Nigeria

The Pulse of the Nation. The Voice of Progress.

Who We Are

Sweep TV Nigeria is a premier digital media platform dedicated to providing comprehensive, unbiased, and up-to-the-minute news coverage with a specialized focus on the All Progressives Congress (APC) and Nigerian grassroots politics.

Founded in Abuja, we have grown to become the most trusted source for party faithfuls, political analysts, and the general public who seek to understand the inner workings of the ruling party and the government's policy directions.

Our Mission

To "sweep" away misinformation by providing factual, evidence-based journalism that empowers Nigerian citizens and promotes democratic accountability through transparent reporting of political activities.

What We Cover

Exclusive APC Updates: Direct access to party conventions, policy shifts, and internal leadership insights.

National Policy Analysis: Breaking down how government decisions affect the common Nigerian.
