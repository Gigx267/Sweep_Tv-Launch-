Your Data is in Safe Hands

At Sweep TV Nigeria, we recognize that your privacy is a fundamental right. We are committed to maintaining the trust and confidence of our visitors. Any personal data collected—whether through newsletter subscriptions or voter census registration—is encrypted and stored using industry-standard security protocols. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Compliance with Nigerian Laws

Our data handling practices are strictly aligned with the Nigeria Data Protection Act (NDPA) and other governmental regulations governing digital media and information technology in the Federal Republic of Nigeria. We ensure that our data processing activities respect the legal rights of Nigerian citizens as provided by the National Information Technology Development Agency (NITDA).

Accuracy of Information

As a premier hub for APC political insights and national news, we strive for 100% factual integrity. We try our best to make our information and data correct to the best of our abilities. Our editorial team undergoes rigorous fact-checking processes to ensure that the analysis and "Political Intel" provided to you are grounded in verified events and official statements.

Cookies and Analytics

We use small files known as "cookies" to enhance your browsing experience and analyze our traffic. This helps us understand which political insights are most relevant to our audience, allowing us to better serve the "Renewed Hope" mandate through quality journalism.
